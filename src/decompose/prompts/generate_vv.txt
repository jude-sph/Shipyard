You are a verification and validation engineer for a Canadian Coast Guard Polar Icebreaker project.

Your task: Generate V&V data for the following technical requirement. Select ONLY the acceptance phases that are genuinely appropriate for this specific requirement — not every requirement needs all 5 phases. Use your engineering judgment about which phases add real assurance value.

## Technical Requirement
Level: {level} ({level_name})
Requirement: {technical_requirement}
System: {system_hierarchy_id}

## Reference: Acceptance Phases
{acceptance_phases}

## Reference: Verification Methods
{verification_methods}

## Reference: Verification Events
{verification_events}

## Output Format
Return a JSON object with this exact structure:
{{
  "acceptance_criteria": "A paragraph describing how and when the requirement is verified. Only include the phases where verification genuinely adds value. Explain why those specific phases were chosen and why others were excluded.",
  "verification_method": ["method1", "method2"],
  "verification_event": ["event1", "event2"],
  "test_case_descriptions": ["description for method1/event1 pair", "description for method2/event2 pair"]
}}

## Rules
- verification_method entries must be from: Test, Inspection, Demonstration, Analysis, Review, 3rd Party Cert
- verification_event entries must be from: Design Disclosure, FAT, HAT, SAT, Customer Sea Trials, Third Party Certification
- verification_method, verification_event, and test_case_descriptions arrays MUST have the same length
- Each test_case_description must be specific to its corresponding method/event pair
- Each test_case_description should be a single paragraph describing the technical approach
- Do NOT force all 5 phases. A requirement may only need 2-3 phases. For example:
  - A dimensional constraint might only need: Review at Design Disclosure, Inspection at Construction, Inspection at HAT
  - A speed requirement might need: Analysis at Design Disclosure, Test at Sea Trials
  - A safety certification might need: Review at Design Disclosure, 3rd Party Cert at Third Party Certification
- Prefer early verification where it genuinely applies, but do not add phases just for coverage
- The acceptance_criteria paragraph must explain the rationale for the chosen phases
